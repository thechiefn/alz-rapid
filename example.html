How to Read This Matrix

  • R — Recommend: Proposes the approach. Gathers data, evaluates options, and presents a recommendation to the Decide role. Typically the person or team closest to the work.
  • A — Agree: Must agree before the decision proceeds. Has veto power — can block, but cannot unilaterally decide. Should be limited to 1–2 stakeholders with critical organizational stake.
  • P — Perform: Executes the work once a decision is made. There can be multiple Perform roles on a single activity across different teams.
  • I — Input: Provides information, analysis, or subject matter expertise. Advisory only — no decision or veto authority. Contributes when asked by the Recommend role.
  • D — Decide: Makes the final call and is accountable for the outcome. There can only be one D per activity.
  • Combined roles (e.g., P,D) indicate a team that both executes and decides for that activity. This is common when a team fully owns a domain end-to-end.
